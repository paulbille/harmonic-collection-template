Your start/main/home page DOES NOT COUNT AS AN ENTRY!

To pass this class your collection needs to have (at a minimum):

  • functioning links to all entries
  • functioning links inside all entries
  • 1 startpage like this one
  • 10 unique entries with at least 1 .html file per entry
  • be responsive: work on mobile phones and desktops
